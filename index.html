TT Automation

A personal desktop tool for posting your own video clips to your own TikTok accounts.

What it does

TT Automation is a personal desktop application used by its owner to upload their own finished video clips to their own TikTok account(s). Before anything is posted, the app shows an on-screen preview of the clip together with the connected account, a privacy-level selector, and interaction settings. The video is only uploaded and published after the owner explicitly confirms it. Nothing is posted without that confirmation.

How it connects to TikTok

The app uses TikTok's official Login Kit so the account owner can authorize their own account, and the Content Posting API to upload the selected video file. It only ever acts on accounts the owner has personally connected.

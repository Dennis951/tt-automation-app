Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

TT Automation ("the app") is a personal desktop tool operated by its owner to post their own video clips to their own TikTok account(s). This policy explains what data the app handles.

Who uses the app

The app is single-user software run locally by its owner. It does not provide accounts or services to other users, and it does not collect data from anyone other than the owner who runs it.

Data the app accesses

  • TikTok account authorization. When the owner connects a TikTok account via TikTok Login Kit, the app receives an access token and a refresh token for that account. These tokens are stored locally on the owner's own computer and are used only to upload videos the owner selects.
  • Basic profile info (user.info.basic). The app reads the connected account's display name and avatar so they can be shown on the confirmation screen before posting. This information is not stored beyond the current session and is not shared.
  • Video uploads (video.upload, video.publish). The app uploads the specific video file the owner chooses and, after the owner confirms, posts it to the connected account.

Data the app does NOT do

  • It does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.
  • It does not run analytics or tracking.
  • It does not store TikTok profile data on any external server — all credentials stay on the owner's local machine.
  • It does not access accounts other than those the owner personally connects.

Data retention & deletion

Access tokens are held locally only for as long as the owner keeps an account connected. The owner can revoke access at any time in their TikTok account settings, and can delete the locally stored tokens by removing the app's local credential file.
